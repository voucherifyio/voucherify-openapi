You can transfer campaigns and tiers between projects to reuse existing configurations.

<Info>
<Badge color="gray">Prerequisites: Required permissions</Badge>

- Transfer permission in the source project  
- Create Campaign permission in the destination project  
</Info>

Transferring a campaign recreates its configuration in the target project. You can choose whether to reuse existing resources or map them to resources in the destination project.

The Transfer option is available in the campaign details view under the three-dot menu.
