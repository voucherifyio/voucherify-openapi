Campaigns and tiers can be **transferred between projects**, allowing you to reuse configurations without recreating them from scratch. This is especially useful when moving setups from staging to production or when standardizing configurations across teams.

When transferring a campaign, Voucherify recreates its configuration in the target project and lets you decide whether to reuse existing resources or map them to ones already available in the destination environment.

The **Transfer** option is available in the Campaign details view under the three-dot menu.

To transfer a campaign or tiers, you need:  
- **Transfer permission** in the source project  
- **Create Campaign permission** in the destination project
