This integration allows you to add a new contact from Autopilot to Voucherify automatically. Plus, you can enhance the profile of a contact being stored with custom properties stored in Autopilot.

## Prerequisites

*   Zapier account 
*   Autopilot account (generated API key for Zapier) 
*   Voucherify account (keys from project settings) 
*   BETA version of Voucherify &lt;&gt; Zapier app - invitation link

## Zapier configuration

1. From the [invitation link](https://zapier.com/developer/invite/62387/cdcdf9275d825dff01c4da836b4c445f/), create a new Zap.

2. Set Trigger: Click the **Create a new Zap** button. In the Trigger Service drop-down, choose Autopilot, and choose a trigger using the additional drop-down – Contact Added.

3. Connect Autopilot account by providing generated access key

4. Test connection by clicking **Test** button.

5. **Set Action**: In the Action Service dropdown, choose Voucherify (invite link) to send your Autopilot data to. In the Action drop-down, choose your desired and click Continue - **Create Customer**

6. **Edit Template for Voucherify action**: Select proper data from Autopilot and forward to Voucherify. The most important: 

*   Source Id - Autopilot contact ID or email.
*   Email - customer email, Customer Name.

7. **Metadata** - custom properties which will be useful for defining distributions and corresponding customer segments: title, company etc. You can use your custom names for properties.

8. **Finalize Your Zap**. Back on your new Zap screen.

9. **Test**: Try out your Zap using the sample/test data shown next to your fields. When you have finished this step, click Continue. As a result of the test, you will see a new customer in Voucherify.

10. **Name Your Zap**: Give your Zap a name. You may find it easiest to use the same Title/Name given to the corresponding feed.

11. **Make it Live**: Click the Make Zap Live button. That's it!
